## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 600g beef mince
- 1 red onion, chopped
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp garam masala
- pinch dried chilli flakes
- 1 cup frozen peas
- ½ cup (125ml) water
- steamed rice, chopped coriander leaves, to garnish

## Directions

1. Heat oil in a large saucepan on high heat. Add beef, cook, without stirring for 2 minutes.
2. Add onion, stir to combine and start to break up the mince with the back of a wooden spoon. Cook until mince is well browned.
3. Add tomatoes and garlic, cook for 2 minutes.
4. Stir in spices, reduce heat to medium, cover and cook for 3-4 minutes.
5. Stir in peas and water, cover, then cook for a further 2 minutes.
6. Serve mince on a bed of steamed rice, garnished with coriander.
