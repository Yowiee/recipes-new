## Ingredients

#### Base layer
- 280 g ground almond or almond flour
- 100 g maple syrup (or clear date syrup)
- 100 g coconut oil
- Pinch of salt

#### Caramel Filling
- 300 g pitted dates
- 150 g pistachio butter
- 240 ml strong black coffee
- 4 tbsp coconut oil
- 1 large pinch of salt adjust to taste
- 1 tbsp vanilla extract
- 100 g of shelled pistachio nuts

#### Chocolate Topping
- 180 g dark chocolate
- 2 tbsp neutral oil

## Directions

1. Soak the pitted dates in hot black coffee to infuse them and to soften the dates.
2. Roughly chop the shelled pistachio nuts.
3. Line a brownie pan or baking tray (approx. 20cm x 30cm) with parchment paper.
4. In a saucepan, melt the coconut oil. In a large bowl, mix the ground almonds with the melted coconut oil and agave syrup or clear date syrup.
5. Pour the mixture into the lined baking tray and compact it with a spoon until it is level and compressed.
6. Place the tray in the freezer for 20 minutes to set while you prepare the caramel filling and melt the chocolate.
7. Drain the dates ensuring to keep the soak water.
8. In a food processor, add all the pistachio caramel ingredients including the coffee and blend until smooth and caramel-like. This may take up to 5 minutes, depending on your food processor. Add more coffee if the mixture is too thick to blend smoothly.
9. Once the base has set, spread the caramel layer evenly over the base.
10. Sprinkle an even layer of chopped pistachio on top so there is a generous coating.
11. Return the tray to the freezer to set again while you melt the chocolate.
12. Melt the chocolate and oil.
13. Carefully spread the chocolate over the caramel.
14. Allow the chocolate to cool and set (at room temperature or in the fridge).
15. Once fully set, cut into squares of your desired size.
